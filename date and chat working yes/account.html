How Does The Eco-Meter Work?

The Eco-Meter

The Eco-Meter is designed to help you track and balance your environmental impact while shopping sustainably. It consists of two progress bars: Eco Points and Waste Points, each reflecting your sustainable shopping behavior. Here's how they work:

Eco Points

The Eco Points visualize your accumulated eco points, which reward you for making sustainable purchasing choices.

How to Earn Eco Points:

  • Eco points are earned based on the waste index of the products in your cart.
  • Products are assigned a waste index based on factors such as the environmental impact of their production, including materials like plastic, pollution from manufacturing, packaging, and other waste-related considerations.
  • Products with a low waste index (under 5) contribute more eco points. For instance, if a product has a waste index of 2, you'll earn (5 - 2) = 3 eco points for each unit purchased.
  • Eco points are capped at 100, so youâ€™ll always have a clear measure of your sustainability efforts.

Benefits of Eco Points:

  • Eco points unlock discounts you can apply during checkout.
  • The more eco points you have, the larger the discount available (up to a maximum of 30% per transaction).
  • Discounts are calculated based on the difference between your eco points and waste points, ensuring that your sustainable behavior directly impacts your rewards.

Waste Points

The Waste Points track your accumulated waste points, which represent the environmental impact of your purchases.

How Waste Points Accumulate:

  • Waste points are calculated using the waste index of the products in your cart. For example, purchasing a product with a waste index of 3 will add 3 waste points for each unit bought.
  • Waste points are capped at 100 to keep your progress measurable.

How to Reduce Waste Points:

  • Waste points can be reduced naturally over time: every 2 days, your waste points will decrease automatically to reflect your ongoing efforts to reduce waste.
  • Purchasing low-waste or zero-waste products helps you maintain a lower waste score.
